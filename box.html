FAQs

Q: How much does it cost to join?
A: For club members, dues are $25 and last till the end of spring semester of that year. For members interested in joining the competitive team, dues are $75.

Q: Do I need to bring anything?
A: Just yourself and some athletic shoes! All dues paying members are also provided with a pair of complementary hand wraps which we encourage you to bring to every practice. Additionally, gloves and mitts are free to use for any club members. You will even see many experienced members bringing their personal equipment.

Q: Will I get hit?
A: Not unless you want to! In the club we practice using mitts and gloves, but do not spar between members. For those interested in sparring and competing, you can try out for the competitive team. Get more information about the competitive team by coming to practice and talking to our coach or a club officer.

Q: Do I need to know anything about boxing?
A: Nope! Terps Boxing Club is open to all skill levels. Whether you've been boxing for the past 10 years or have never picked up a pair of running shoes, we are here to provide a welcoming environment for all experience levels.

Q: What are the workouts like?
A: Our workouts are very intense and aim to get all members into top physical shape. Every practice focuses on cardio, intense calisthenics, and boxing technique, yet no practice is exactly the same.
